bEngo

bEngo is a version of Bingo, but instead of numbers from 1 to 90 the cards have the numbers of the food additives as European standard, the misterious E-numbers.

I am writing the bash script to pull the common name of the additives and their purpose in bash: code. I have still to understand how to retrieve the purpose of the additives efficiently. Then, I will make a table with random E-numbers (in R?) to print the cards for the game.
