A bit about me

I am Italian following the route of Goths moved to Germany after completeing my PhD in Denmark. I obtained a BSc in Biology and a MSc in Biology (Ecology) from the University of Florence. I moved to Denmark in 2017 to pursue a PhD in Ecology, which I obtained in June 2020: diploma. I moved to Germany soon after to start a postdoc research position.

I am very fascinated by ancient history of the Mediterranean basin, in particular of Greek and Roman civilizations and of that mysterious people that called themselves Rasenna, who appeared from nowhere to rule on the hills of Tuscany and then to slowly fade into oblivion. I strongly believe that the Anabasis by Xenohpon will be a great hit on Netflix soon.
