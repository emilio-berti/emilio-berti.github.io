R packages

Enerscape

I authored the R package enerscape, which computes energy landscapes of terrestrial animals (and of cyclists). Here's a vignette showing some example case studies.

I used enerscape also to study elephants preferences for costly terrain in Samburu. I used the continous Boyce index and GPS records to obtain a general preference of individuals for areas of given energetic costs of travel. An example on how to obtain this can be found here.
