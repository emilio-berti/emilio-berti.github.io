R packages

enerscape

I authored the R package enerscape, which computes energy landscapes of terrestrial animals (and of cyclists). Here's a vignette showing some example case studies.

I used enerscape also to study elephants preferences for costly terrain in Samburu. I used the continous Boyce index and GPS records to obtain a general preference of individuals for areas of given energetic costs of travel. An example on how to obtain this can be found here.

ATNr

I helped developing the R package ATNr, which implements allometric trophic network models for consumer-resource communities to predict biomass dynamics of species. The package vignette can be found here.

assembly

I am working on the assembly package right now. Assembly of ecological community is still an unsolved problem. With this package, I want to provide a user-friendly way to simulate several top-down assembly processes, namely filtering by resource availability and by limiting similarity.
